Root Causes of Incoherence

The absence of 'resonance' leads to specific failures. The primary cause is a 'Lack of Grounding,' an inability to connect symbolic data to real-world facts. This is followed by 'Contextual Misalignment' and 'Overconfident Inferences' where the model confidently fabricates information.

These issues highlight that current mitigation techniques are treating the symptoms, not the underlying architectural cause.
